Something I find interesting recently is the positive mental impact that comes with playing golf. Most people may consider the sport to be a physical game, but there is much more to just swinging a golf club. Many factors play a role in getting that Perfect Swing such as your grip, backswing, stance, form, and even what clubs and grips you are using! Because there are so many techniques that someone must know to properly play golf, I find it to be rather soothing. Using the sport as an outlet from the outside world is my form of therapy. And I have also developed a love-hate relationship with the game of golf!
